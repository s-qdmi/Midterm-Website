Retiarius

Nation of Origin: Thrace

Description: The Retiarius is one of the most unique and strategic gladiators in ancient Roman combat. Known for their agility, speed, and intelligence, the Retiarius is a fighter who specializes in using a trident (a three-pronged spear), a net (or rete), and light armor to outmaneuver and trap opponents. Unlike heavily armored gladiators, the Retiarius relies on his quick reflexes and tactical mind to dominate in the arena.

Price: 100 Gold Coins
