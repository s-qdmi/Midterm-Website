Provocator

Nation of Origin: Rome

Description: The Provocator is one of the most powerful and imposing types of gladiators in ancient Rome. Characterized by their heavy armor, large shield, and short sword (gladius), the Provocator is a fighter who excels in direct confrontation, offensive attacks, and overpowering opponents through brute strength and resilience. Unlike other gladiators who may rely on agility or finesse, the Provocator relies on their heavy armor to absorb blows while closing the gap between themselves and their adversaries.

Price: 150 Gold Coins
