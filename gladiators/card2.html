Hoplomachus

Nation of Origin: Greece

Description: The Hoplomachus is one of the most well-known and respected gladiator types in ancient Rome. Originating from Greek warfare, the Hoplomachus is a heavily armored, well-trained warrior, known for their ability to engage in close-quarters combat with precision and discipline. With a distinctive short spear (or hasta), shield (parma), and helmet, the Hoplomachus is a skilled combatant whose strategy revolves around solid defense, quick thrusting attacks, and overcoming opponents through endurance and strength.
